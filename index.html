Frequently asked questions

Are pinball machines still being manufactured?

Yes. There’s been a strong resurgence, with multiple new titles released each year.

Where are new machines made?

Most major manufacturers are in the U.S., with others appearing worldwide.

How much does a machine cost?

Pricing varies widely by model and edition. As a rough guide, new “home” editions start lower, while premium and limited runs can cost significantly more.

Where can I play?

Check the interactive map above. We keep it as current as possible.

Do you sell your machines?

We focus on keeping them in public so more people can play. If you’re seeking a specific title, contact us and we’ll try to help.
